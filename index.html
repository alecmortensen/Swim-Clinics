Our Story

Swim Clinics was born from a simple frustration: watching companies take massive cuts from hard-working instructors while providing minimal value. As a swim instructor for years, I saw firsthand how the traditional model left both instructors and students shortchanged.

Instructors were making $45/hour while companies charged students $100+ per lesson. The math didn't add up, and neither did the ethics. Students deserved better pricing, and instructors deserved fair compensation for their expertise.

We're building a platform that puts instructors in control and gives students direct access to elite talent. Fair pricing. Transparent marketplace. Better swimming education for everyone.
